PHASE II BACK-OF-TUBE IMAGE
==========================

To show the back of the Phase II tube in the product slideshow:

1. Find your image file (the photo of the back of the tube).

2. Copy that file.

3. Rename the copy to exactly:
   phase-ii-tube-back.png

4. Put it in THIS folder (the same folder as this file).
   Full path:
   c:\Users\talis\Downloads\Kimi_Agent_React Site Cart Checkout (6)\app\public\

5. Restart your dev server (stop it and run "npm run dev" again in the app folder).

Done. When you open Phase II and click the arrow to see the second slide, your image will appear.
